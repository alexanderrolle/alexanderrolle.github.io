I am a TUM Global Postdoc Fellow at The Technical University of Munich working with Ulrich Bauer. Before that I was a postdoc with Michael Kerber at TU Graz, and before that I did a PhD supervised by Rick Jardine at The University of Western Ontario.

Research interests

My main research interests are in applied and computational topology, especially applications to statistics and data analysis.
