Alexander Rolle

Short biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Mathematics department at The Technical University of Munich, working with Ulrich Bauer. From September 2019 to September 2021 I was a postdoc with Michael Kerber at TU Graz, Austria. In August 2019 I finished a PhD in Mathematics at The University of Western Ontario, Canada, supervised by Rick Jardine.

Research interests

My research interests are in applied topology, especially applications to statistics, data analysis, and machine learning.
